# Foreign Direct Investment Policy

## E-Commerce Rules
An entity having equity participation by e-commerce marketplace entity or its group companies, or having control on its inventory by e-commerce marketplace entity or its group companies, will not be permitted to sell its products on the platform run by such marketplace entity.

## Beneficial Ownership
Where the investee company is owned or controlled by non-resident entities, the downstream investment by such company shall be subject to the same conditions and sectoral caps as applicable to foreign investment. Ownership is determined by beneficial ownership of more than 50% of the equity.
