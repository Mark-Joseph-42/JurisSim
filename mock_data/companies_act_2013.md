# Companies Act, 2013

## Section 2(46): Holding Company
"Holding company", in relation to one or more other companies, means a company of which such companies are subsidiary companies.

## Section 2(76): Related Party
"Related party" with reference to a company means a director or his relative, a key managerial personnel or his relative, a firm or private company in which a director is a partner or member, a public company in which a director holds more than two percent of its paid-up share capital, or any body corporate whose Board of Directors or manager is accustomed to act in accordance with the advice of a director or manager.

## Section 2(87): Subsidiary Company
"Subsidiary company" means a company in which the holding company controls the composition of the Board of Directors, or exercises or controls more than one-half of the total voting power.

## Section 135: Corporate Social Responsibility
Every company having net worth of Rs 500 crore or more, or turnover of Rs 1000 crore or more, or net profit of Rs 5 crore or more during the immediately preceding financial year shall constitute a CSR Committee and spend at least 2% of its average net profits on CSR activities.

## Section 447: Fraud
Any person who is found to be guilty of fraud shall be punishable with imprisonment for a term not less than six months but which may extend to ten years and shall also be liable to fine which shall not be less than the amount involved in the fraud, but which may extend to three times the amount involved in the fraud.
