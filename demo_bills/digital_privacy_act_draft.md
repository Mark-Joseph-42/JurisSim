# Draft Digital Privacy and Accountability Act, 2026

## Section 2(a): Data Controller
"Data Controller" means any entity registered under the Companies Act, 2013, that collects, stores, or processes personal data of Indian citizens through servers physically located within the territory of India.

## Section 2(b): Personal Data
"Personal Data" means any data that directly identifies a natural person, including name, Aadhaar number, PAN, and biometric data.

## Section 4: Consent
A Data Controller must obtain verifiable consent from the data subject before processing any personal data.

## Section 5: Breach Notification
A Data Controller must notify the Data Protection Board within 72 hours of becoming aware of a personal data breach.

## Section 6: Right to Erasure
Upon request by the data subject, a Data Controller must erase all personal data within 30 days.

## Section 8: Data Localisation
All personal data of Indian citizens must be stored on servers physically located within India.

## Section 12: Penalties
Any Data Controller found in violation of this Act shall be liable to a penalty not exceeding fifty crore rupees per instance.
